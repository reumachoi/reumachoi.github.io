# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit **reumachoi.github.io** (the "Site").

### 1. Personal Information We Collect
We do not directly collect personal information such as names or email addresses unless you voluntarily provide them (e.g., via comments or email). However, like most websites, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, and time zone.

### 2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising
We use **Google AdSense** to serve ads when you visit our Site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. 
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site.
- You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### 3. Google Analytics
We use **Google Analytics** to understand how our visitors use the Site. You can read more about how Google uses your Personal Information here: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/).

### 4. Contact Us
For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, please contact us by e-mail at **reumaco99@gmail.com**.
